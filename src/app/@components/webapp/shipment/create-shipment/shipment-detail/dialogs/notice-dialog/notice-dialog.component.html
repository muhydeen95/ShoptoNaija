Important Shipment Notice!

To proceed with your shipment, please follow these steps carefully:

Step 1: Check Your Email

After clicking the "Create Shipment" button, an email will be sent to you. Please check your inbox to see if your shipment has been approved or rejected.

Step 2: Approval Status

  • If your shipment is Approved, you can move to the next step.

  • If your shipment is Rejected, review the reasons provided in the email and retry

Step 3: Proceed To Payment

If approved, go to the Shipment Tab on the side navigation menu to complete your payment and finalize your shipment process.
